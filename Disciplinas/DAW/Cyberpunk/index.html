What is Cyberpunk?

Cyberpunk is a distopian that envolves NeoCapitalism and Mega Corporations rulling the world. Not so far that we live now.

A cyberpunk city has propaganda everywhere: in social media, search sites, payed games, buiulding holograms, etc

Everything is ruled by money. The social inequality is more than never and the corporations want this way. People have to work exausting turnships to a miserable salary and even be exposed to consume everything all time.
